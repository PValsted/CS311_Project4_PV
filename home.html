Welcome to the "Great Composers" website. Whether you are an avid fan of music in general or just want to learn some fascinating history, you've come to the right place. According to the English National Opera website, "Opera (the Italian word for ‘work’) is an art form that tells a story through music and singing. Unlike a musical, opera singers do not use microphones to amplify their voices, and the music, played by the orchestra, is completely live."

This site details briefly the life of five great composers of opera, listing their works to the side as well. Please use the above navigation bar to read about these talented and noteworthy artists. Happy learning!
